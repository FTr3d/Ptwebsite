Who we are

Prestige Travels is a bespoke luxury travel agency based in Amsterdam. We specialize in curating exceptional journeys to any destination in the world. We are a small, dedicated team of travel experts who believe that booking a holiday should be as stress-free as the holiday itself.

We don't use algorithms or automated booking engines. Every itinerary is designed by a human advisor who takes the time to understand your preferences, pace, and travel style.

What drives us

Personal First

You are not a booking reference number. You have a dedicated advisor from your first enquiry until you return home safely.

Zero Pressure

We provide transparent quotes and unlimited revisions. We will never pressure you to commit before you are completely satisfied.

Expertise

We only sell what we know. Our advisors have personally visited every resort and destination we offer.

Community

We work with trusted local partners to ensure your experiences are authentic, responsible, and of the highest quality.

Our story

Prestige Travels was founded in Amsterdam with a single belief: that travel planning should be as enjoyable as the trip itself. We are a small, dedicated agency — and that's exactly how we intend to stay.

We don't grow by volume. We grow by reputation. Every client we work with becomes part of our community, and every trip we build reflects a genuine understanding of what exceptional travel feels like.
