How our payment plans work

When you confirm a booking with Prestige Travels, we offer a milestone-based payment plan so you can spread the cost over time. There is no interest, no admin fee, and your total price never changes — regardless of how many instalments you make.

The exact payment schedule depends on how far in advance you book. All milestones are confirmed in writing before you pay your deposit.

Departure Window Deposit (within 5 days) Milestone 1 Milestone 2 Final (28 days before)
Over 12 months 25% 50% at 9 months 75% at 3 months 100%
6–12 months 25% 50% at 3 months — 100%
3–6 months 25% 75% at 6 weeks — 100%
Under 3 months 50% — — 100%
Under 28 days 100% at booking — — —

All cancellation charges are calculated on the full Travel Sum, not just the amount paid so far.

Availability & right to refuse

Payment plans are not guaranteed for every booking. We reserve the right to decline or withdraw a payment plan at our discretion. This will always be communicated clearly before you confirm your booking.

Missed payments

If a payment milestone is missed, your advisor will contact you proactively. A 14-day grace period applies from the original due date. Repeated non-payment beyond the grace period may, in rare cases, result in the booking being released. Full terms are included in your booking confirmation.
